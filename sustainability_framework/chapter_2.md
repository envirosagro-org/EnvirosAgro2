# Chapter 2: Agricultural Sustainability Fundamentals

Agricultural sustainability refers to the capacity of farming systems to maintain productivity while conserving environmental resources and supporting socio-economic stability.

Key fundamentals include:

* **Soil health** as the foundation of plant growth
* **Water availability and efficiency**
* **Biodiversity and ecosystem services**
* **Climate resilience**

EnvirosAgro emphasizes that sustainability is dynamic. Agricultural systems operate within ecological timeframes, meaning sustainability must be assessed across seasons, years, and generations rather than as a one-time condition.
