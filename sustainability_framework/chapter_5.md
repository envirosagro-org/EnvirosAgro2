# Chapter 5: Input Parameters Definition

Each sustainability model relies on defined parameters:

### **5.1 Crop Cycle Requirement (S)**

Represents the time needed for a crop to complete its growth cycle under normal conditions.

### **5.2 Direct Nature Factor (Dₙ)**

Measures immediate natural inputs such as rainfall days or sunlight exposure.

### **5.3 Indirect Nature Factor (Iₙ)**

Accounts for stored or delayed resources such as soil moisture retention or groundwater availability.

### **5.4 Agricultural Base Factor (x)**

A scaled measure (e.g., 1–10) representing the level of sustainable practice integration.

### **5.5 Growth / Adoption Rate (r)**

Indicates how rapidly sustainability practices are adopted or improved over time.

### **5.6 Number of Periods (n)**

Defines the duration of analysis, such as seasons or years.
