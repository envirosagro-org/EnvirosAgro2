# Chapter 4: Time-Series Sustainability Modeling

Time-series modeling enables sustainability assessment across multiple periods. Instead of static evaluation, EnvirosAgro tracks how sustainability evolves due to:

* Changes in climate patterns
* Technology adoption
* Resource management decisions

This approach allows users to:

* Predict future sustainability performance
* Identify declining or improving trends
* Compare scenarios over time

Time-series sustainability modeling is especially valuable in climate-variable regions and long-cycle crops.
