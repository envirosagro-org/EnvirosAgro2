# Chapter 1: Introduction to EnvirosAgro Sustainability

EnvirosAgro Sustainability is founded on the principle that agriculture is both a scientific discipline and a human–nature relationship system. It recognizes that food production depends not only on inputs and technology but also on ecological balance, time, and adaptive decision-making.

This framework positions sustainability as a measurable and manageable process rather than an abstract concept. By integrating time-based metrics, natural resource availability, and adoption of sustainable practices, EnvirosAgro provides a structured approach to evaluating agricultural resilience and long-term productivity.

The primary objective of this framework is to enable farmers, policymakers, agribusinesses, and researchers to quantify sustainability performance, simulate future outcomes, and make evidence-based decisions.
